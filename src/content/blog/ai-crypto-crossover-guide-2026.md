## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Treating AI signals as trading signals**: AI that analyzes crypto data does not predict price movements. It processes information faster, not more accurately, than markets.
- **Automating without manual override capability**: Any automation that manages real funds needs a clear way to pause or exit. Smart contracts that can't be exited are not appropriate for passive income strategies.
- **Ignoring gas costs in automation**: A Make.com alert is cheap; an on-chain agent rebalancing every 6 hours on Ethereum mainnet is expensive. Model transaction costs before deploying automation.
- **Outsourcing security decisions to AI**: No AI correctly evaluates the security of a new smart contract. Use established, audited protocols — AI can help research audit status but can't replace your judgment on novel protocols.
- **Neglecting tax tracking**: Active DeFi users who don't track from the start face enormous retroactive reconstruction problems.
