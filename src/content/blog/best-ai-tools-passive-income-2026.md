**1. Content Creation at Scale**
Blog posts, YouTube videos, newsletters. AI handles the heavy lifting of production so you can publish more frequently without burning out. The income comes from ads, sponsorships, and affiliate commissions.

Think client onboarding, social media scheduling, data entry, lead generation. You build the workflow once, then it runs on autopilot. The income comes from selling the automation as a service or freeing your time to take on more clients.

You take on copywriting, design, or video editing gigs but use AI to do the work 5x faster. Your hourly rate effectively multiplies. Not purely passive, but it builds capital for truly passive ventures.

**4. Digital Products**
Courses, templates, ebooks, calculators. AI helps you create the product faster. Once it's built, it sells on repeat with minimal maintenance.

The sweet spot? Combining two or three of these models with the right AI passive income automation stack. That's where things get interesting.
